About Us

A Journey to Sustainability and Community Empowerment

Sharon Farm is an integrated urban farm located in Quezon City, dedicated to promoting sustainable agriculture and improving food security in local communities.

Covering 5,500 square meters of urban land, the farm is a green space where nature and innovation work together. We use eco-friendly farming methods like aquaponics, vertical gardening, and organic pest control to grow healthy food while protecting the environment. The farm also serves as a learning space for the community like for women's groups, returning OFWs, and other local organizations.

We work closely with the Department of Agriculture, PSIA, and local government units to run programs that support education, skill-building, and community involvement. One of our key initiatives is a barter system, where people can exchange recyclables like plastic and glass for fresh vegetables.

At Sharon Farm, our mission is to grow not just crops, but a greener, more self-reliant future for everyone.

How to Partner With Sharon Farm

At Sharon Farm, we are committed to promoting sustainable agriculture and community empowerment. We invite individuals, organizations, and businesses to collaborate with us in advancing our mission of environmental responsibility, education, and community support.

Support Our Barter System

Our barter program allows local community members to exchange recyclable materials such as glass, plastic, and other reusable items for fresh vegetables. This initiative encourages environmental responsibility and strengthens community bonds. Partners can contribute by:

  • Donating recyclable materials to support the program
  • Sponsoring fresh produce for exchange in the barter system
  • Assisting in scaling the program to serve more people

Assist in Sustainable Farming Practices

Sharon Farm faces challenges such as managing natural predators and using eco-friendly pest control methods instead of chemical pesticides. We seek support to enhance these sustainable practices. Ways to assist include:

  • Providing or donating beneficial insects for natural pest control
  • Offering expertise and training on organic and sustainable farming techniques

Contribute to Farm Expansion and Growth

To further our impact, we aim to expand our operations and increase access to resources. We have already received valuable support from the Department of Science and Technology (DOST). Additional support can be provided by:

  • Donating modern farming equipment to improve production
  • Offering technical training and knowledge transfer
  • Assisting with expanding market access and distribution channels

Opportunities for Investment and Collaboration

We are actively seeking:

  • Investors interested in supporting sustainable urban agriculture
  • Strategic partners who can contribute resources or knowledge
  • Membership in cooperatives or farmer networks
