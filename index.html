About Us

At Sharon Farm, the heart of our mission lies in community engagement where sustainability meets collaboration. Through a unique barter system, locals are encouraged to exchange recyclable materials such as glass, plastic, and other reusable items in return for fresh vegetables. This initiative not only promotes environmental responsibility but also strengthens community bonds through shared values of health, sustainability, and mutual support Join us in building a greener, self-sufficient community!

Projects

  • Community Model Farm - GrowQC Food Security Program

    "Sharon Farm aids local feeding initiatives as a component of the Barangay Kontra Gutom effort within the GrowQC Food Security Program."

  • Integration of Modern Farming Techniques

    "Sharon Farm employs drip irrigation, aquaponics, and composting biodigesters to encourage sustainable and efficient urban agriculture."

  • Livelihood and Skills Training Programs

    "Sharon Farm provides education on composting and the Kadiwa Palit Kalakal system, enabling individuals to engage in sustainable urban agriculture practices."

  • Community Engagement and Educational Activities

    "Sharon Farm organizes community gatherings and educational activities, providing students with practical experiences in sustainable urban farming methods."
